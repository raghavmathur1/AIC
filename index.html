Our Story
In line with the pursuits of Government of India towards Make in India & Start-Up India, the Technology & Business Incubation Center at SMIT started as a Satellite Center of Manipal Institute of Technology & Business Incubator, Manipal with an MOU from January 2015. The Center incubates, arranges funds and nurtures early-stage entrepreneurial ventures based on Technology and Innovation by providing infrastructure, consultancy, and seed grants to promising entrepreneurs to pursue their dreams of starting their own firm. The centre has persistently encouraged young entrepreneurs to hone their skills in generating Values Human Resources and provide solutions rather than the traditional Man Power that feeds the needs of the industry.

The incubation centre admits young entrepreneurs with innovative ideas that have market potential and guides them to become employers rather than being employees until they graduate from this centre after a gestation period of approximately 3 years. The incubation centre believes in providing the platform for entrepreneurs to launch their startups and provide solutions to problems of the 21st century. We as incubation centre focus on creating an environment of freedom for new and young entrepreneurs, to help them develop their startups to self-sustaining companies.
