Mr. Tshewang Topden
Retired IFS officer/Ambassador/Diplomat Entrepreneurship development, Strategic planning and industry-government Liaoning.
Mr. Topden is a retired I.F.S Officer and a former diplomat. He has been Consul General in Frankfurt, Ambassador to Vientiane (Laos), High Commissioner in Windhoek (Namibia), Ambassador in Athens (Greece) among other postings. As the Indian Ambassador in Laos and he helped the government there to set up the Indo -Lao Entrepreneurship Centre, which was a great success. As an Ambassador in other places and diplomat, he has assisted many Indian companies to set up business in the countries where he was posted and also help companies from those countries to set up business in India and to invest.
Mr. Rajen Padukone
Group President of the Manipal Education & Medical Group (Manipal Group)
Mr Rajen Padukone's varied experience of over 30 years, includes appointments at ANZ Grindlays Bank, Crompton Greaves Limited and Kirloskar Oil Engines Ltd and covers the healthcare, education, investment banking, international finance, retail financial services and manufacturing sectors. Mr Padukone joined the Manipal Group in 1998 and over the past 14 years has worked in both their health as well as their education verticals. His healthcare experience includes establishing start-ups, operations and building & linking healthcare networks across the spectrum of primary to tertiary care. His experience is the area of education includes managing various initiatives from campus based education to distance and distributed learning and corporate & continuing education.
